Advanced HR Leave Restriction with Mandatory Days Odoo App

Our all-inclusive Odoo – Advanced HR Leave Restriction with Mandatory Days Odoo App for Odoo simplifies and enforces company leave policies by allowing organizations to define restricted dates and mandatory leave days directly within the HR system. HR administrators can configure critical business periods—such as peak workloads, audits, or company events—during which leave requests are automatically blocked or enforced according to company rules. Leave requests are validated in real time, ensuring full compliance while reducing manual HR intervention. All settings are easily managed from the Odoo backend without any technical expertise. By improving planning visibility and preventing policy violations, this app helps organizations maintain operational continuity and deliver a transparent, well-controlled leave management experience.

  • Define specific dates as mandatory working days to prevent leave requests during critical periods.
  • Apply leave restrictions selectively to individual employees or employee groups based on company rules.
  • Instantly notify users when a leave request violates mandatory day or restriction rules.
  • Display clear pop-up notifications on the dashboard to highlight mandatory or restricted leave days.

Complete Workflow for Manage Advanced HR Leave Restriction with Mandatory Days Odoo App

First, go to Time Off -> Configuration -> Mandatory Days and configure mandatory days by selecting specific employees and a date range. Once saved, these mandatory days are highlighted with pop-up alerts in the calendar view. When an employee attempts to apply leave on a mandatory day, the system automatically restricts the request. A real-time warning message is displayed to prevent policy violations.
